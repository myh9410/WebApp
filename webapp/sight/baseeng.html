Traditional Palace Course

Attractions

  • Changgyeong Palace
  • Changdeok Palace
  • Gyeongbok Palace
Course
Changgyeong Palace > Changdeok Palace > Gyeongbok Palace or reverse
Explanation
A course where you can see the old palaces of the Joseon Dynasty. The route is Changgyeong Palace, Changdeok Palace and Gyeongbok Palace as recommended above. To use time efficiently, take a look Changdeokgung and Changgyeonggung and have lunch. Going to Gyeongbokgung palaces after that will be good for time use. The palaces do not open at night except in summer, so if you can see it from morning, you can look around more. It is a course where you can make good memories by wearing a hanbok and walking through palaces.
